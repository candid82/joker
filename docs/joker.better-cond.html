  • cond

    Macro v1.0 
    (cond & clauses)

    A variation on cond which sports let bindings, do and implicit else:
    (cond
    (odd? a) 1
    :do (println a)
    :let [a (quot a 2)]
    (odd? a) 2
    3).
    Also supports :when-let and :when-some.
    :let, :when-let, :when-some and :do do not need to be written as keywords.
